Wiring Diagram

Black - Ground
White - Vcc [5-6vdc]
Grey - Pin 13 - CL [Strip Clock]
Purple - Pin 11- DI [Strip Data]
Blue - Pin 6 - Orientation PWM Input
Green - Pin 4 - Frame Rate PWM Input
Yellow - Pin 2 - Message Select PWM Input


BlueTooth Module

BT - Nano v3
Vcc - 3v3
Gnd - Gnd
TxD - D8
RxD - D7 (use a voltage divider to bring it down to 3v)
Voltage Divider: D7 - 1K - (to module) - 2K - Gnd
